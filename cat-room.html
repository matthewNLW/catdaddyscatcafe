Common Questions

Cat Room FAQs

Do I need to wear Socks?
Yes, we request no shoes in the cat room to keep it clean but you must wear socks. If you don't bring socks you can buy a pair of cat socks in our shop.
How many cats are there?
12 cats
How much does it cost to go in the cat room?
$10 for 30 minutes. On weekends we offer 1 hour appointments for $15.
Do you have to make a reservation?
No, we accept walk-ins but it is recommended during our busy hours which is usually afternoons and weekends.
Where did you get the cats?
Most were found in the neighborhood of NOMA by Al Amir or brought to Mohammed by their mothers to get help with their kittens.
Do we adopt out our cats?
No, our cafe cats are residents here at Cat Daddy Cat Cafe. Unlike some other cat cafes in the U.S. But no worries! We will host adoption days for you to come and find your new furry friend!
When will we have adoption days?
Once or twice a month we will host adoption days. Keep an eye out on our social media for all updates regarding adoption days!
Can you have birthday parties here?
Yes! Please call ahead so we can book your party.
Can I make donations?
Depending on what it is you’re interesting in donating, please give us a call. We accept toys as long as they are unused.
