Our Story

The Heart of the Cafe.

Welcome to Cat Daddy Cat Cafe, where our love for cats and community comes together!

The Origin

Our journey began in the most unexpected way, thanks to three curious stray cats who found their way into Mohammad's heart.

These feline wanderers started following him around the neighborhood, and seeing their hunger, he couldn't resist feeding them. Soon, Mohammad's trio of furry friends grew into a lively gang.

Determined to make a difference, they started bringing the stray cats in. And that's how Cat Daddy Cat Cafe was born — a place where rescued cats find a loving home and visitors get to enjoy their company!
